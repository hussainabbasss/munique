# Munique 2026 Conference Platform

## Register

**brand** — The public conference website is a marketing surface where design IS the product. Admin dashboard and registration portal are product register (design serves the workflow).

## Users & Purpose

- **Delegates** arriving from Instagram on mobile — need institutional credibility and a clear Register path within seconds.
- **Executive Board** — operational control via admin dashboard (separate surfaces).
- **Sponsors & partners** — professional public presence reflecting Munique's credibility.

## Brand Personality

Ceremonial, institutional, confident. A diplomatic dossier — parchment and ink, sealed and official. Not a school club poster or generic MUN template.

## Anti-References

- AI cream/sand body backgrounds with terracotta accents
- Generic SaaS card grids, pill buttons, glassmorphism nav
- Stock UN/diplomacy hero photography
- Checkmark status systems (the Seal is the status language)
- Dark-mode neon marketing pivots

## Strategic Design Principles

1. **Emblem-first** — the seal is the hero, not stock imagery.
2. **Document desk layout** — index cards, hairline rules, not identical icon cards.
3. **Ceremonial restraint** — one Anton display line per viewport max; stamp animation reserved for registration confirmation.
4. **Identity preservation** — extend Instagram-established parchment/ink/gold palette, do not replace.

## Primary Surface

Homepage at `/` — public front door routing to registration and conference information.
